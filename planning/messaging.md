# PLANNING: Q2 Messaging Update

## Problem
Current pricing page copy ("Plans and Pricing") tested at 3.2/5 clarity score in March user research sessions (n=42). Users described it as "generic" and "doesn't tell me what makes this different." Conversion rate on pricing page is 2.1%, below 3.5% benchmark.

## Hypothesis
Updating the pricing page headline and subheadline to emphasize simplicity and transparency will increase pricing page conversion rate by 10%+ vs control.

## Scope
- Update pricing page headline from "Plans and Pricing" to "Simple, Transparent Pricing"
- Update subheadline from "Choose the plan that's right for you" to "No hidden fees. No surprises. Switch or cancel anytime."
- Copy changes only — no layout, design, or pricing tier changes

## Non-Goals
- Redesigning the pricing page layout
- Changing actual pricing or tier structure
- Updating pricing in marketing site or emails (separate initiative)

## Success Metrics
- **Primary:** Pricing page conversion rate increases ≥10% vs control (baseline: 2.1%)
- **Guardrail:** Bounce rate doesn't increase by more than 2%
- **Guardrail:** Support tickets about pricing confusion don't increase

## Rollout Plan
- **Exposure:** 50% of pricing page visitors
- **Duration:** 4 weeks
- **Randomization:** User-level (cookie-based)
- **Ramp gates:** Conversion rate ≥ 2.3% after week 1
- **Kill criteria:** Roll back if conversion drops below 1.8% or bounce rate increases >5%

## Dependencies
- User research report from March sessions (completed)
- Brand team sign-off on new copy (approved 2026-03-28)
